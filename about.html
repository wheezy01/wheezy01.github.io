About AWFC Irish Gooners

We are a passionate community of Irish fans based in Ireland and the UK. Our admins work hard to keep this community strong and connected.

Meet the Admins

Alison

Alison, one of the founders of the Irish Women's Supporters Club, is the heart and soul of the group. Her quick wit and hilarious one-liners never fail to bring laughter to everyone around her. Always the first to break the ice, Alison’s humor creates a welcoming and fun atmosphere for all members. Beyond her great sense of humor, she’s also a passionate organizer, ensuring regular meet-ups that bring the group closer together. Her enthusiasm and dedication make her an invaluable part of the community.

Emer

Emer, another key founder of the Irish Women's Supporters Club, is the driving force behind the club's vibrant online presence. As the social media manager, she keeps everyone in the loop with regular updates on Instagram, from match details to starting line-ups, making sure no one misses a moment. Her dedication to keeping the group informed and engaged is unmatched. However, when she's not managing the club's socials, you’ll often find her browsing Arsenal Direct—her love for the team occasionally leading to some serious splurges on the latest gear!

Grace

Grace, the club's passionate helper from across the pond in Dublin, plays an essential role in encouraging everyone to get involved. Her enthusiasm extends beyond just supporting Arsenal Women—she's always motivating the group to rally behind the Irish soccer team as well. With her unwavering support and infectious spirit, Grace inspires everyone to broaden their love for the sport and cheer for their teams with pride, both locally and internationally.

Ally

Ally, a proud Irish supporter and member of the Irish Arsenal Supporters Group, brings her infectious passion for football to the community. A massive fan of the Republic of Ireland Women's National Team, Ally keeps the group buzzing with her entertaining football content and lively match discussions. Whether she’s sharing match predictions or reacting to the latest Arsenal and Ireland women's games, Ally’s enthusiasm never goes unnoticed. And when she's not creating content, you’ll likely find her leading the charge in creating chants at games, making sure the atmosphere is always electric!

Join Us!

If you are a passionate Arsenal fan living in Ireland or a Gooner looking to connect with fellow fans, we’d love to have you! Get in touch with us via the Contact Us page, and let’s make this community stronger.
